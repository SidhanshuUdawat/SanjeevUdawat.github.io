Vedic astrology, also known as Jyotish

Is an ancient Indian system of astrology that dates back thousands of years. It relies on the positions of celestial bodies in relation to fixed stars and uses a unique zodiac system known as the sidereal zodiac. Vedic astrology is deeply rooted in Hindu philosophy and is used for various purposes, including personal guidance, marriage compatibility, and life predictions.

Vastu Shastra is often referred to as "Indian Feng Shui" or simply "Vastu"

Is a traditional Indian architectural and design system that focuses on creating harmonious and balanced living spaces. It prescribes principles and guidelines for the layout, construction, and arrangement of buildings to promote positive energy flow and well-being. Vastu Shastra takes into account factors like direction, placement of rooms, and materials to enhance the quality of life and prosperity of its occupants.
